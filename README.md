# Analyze-Operational-Data-to-Improve-Logistics-Warehousing-Efficiency-Logi-Tech

Analyzed large-scale logistics and supply chain data to improve delivery performance, warehouse efficiency, inventory management, and cost optimization. The project focused on identifying operational inefficiencies and enabling data-driven decision-making across end-to-end logistics processes.

Leveraged Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn) for data analysis and machine learning, MySQL for complex querying and KPI extraction, and Power BI for building interactive dashboards. Conducted EDA, statistical analysis, hypothesis testing, and predictive modeling to uncover insights and forecast risks.

Key analysis areas included delivery reliability, transportation cost, warehouse utilization, inventory health, turnover performance, and service quality metrics. Identified high-cost and low-efficiency warehouses, underutilized capacity, and carrier performance gaps affecting delivery accuracy and damage rates.

Developed machine learning models (Logistic Regression & KNN) to predict delivery accuracy and shipment risk, achieving over 99% accuracy, enabling proactive decision-making.

Provided actionable recommendations such as carrier optimization, warehouse load balancing, cost control strategies, and predictive risk monitoring, leading to improved operational efficiency and reduced logistics costs.
